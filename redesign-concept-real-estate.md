# Website Redesign Concept — Real Estate Agent

## Concept Redesign — Not affiliated with business

---

## Business Type
**Real Estate Agent / Realtor** (Residential, commercial, or luxury real estate)

---

## Common Problems with Current Real Estate Agent Websites

- **Generic templates** that don't stand out from competitors
- **No property search functionality** — forces users to third-party sites
- **Outdated listings** that don't update automatically
- **Poor mobile experience** — 76% of home searches start on mobile
- **No lead capture** system for serious buyers/sellers
- **Missing neighborhood info** that home buyers want
- **No mortgage calculator** or affordability tools
- **No way to schedule showings** online
- **Weak social proof** — missing testimonials and market data
- **No SEO for "near me" searches** or neighborhood-specific terms
- **Slow loading** property galleries frustrate users
- **No CRM integration** to track leads

---

## Redesign Strategy

### Brand Positioning
Position the agent as the neighborhood's trusted real estate expert — someone who knows the local market inside and out and provides personalized service that big brokerages can't match.

### Key Strategic Elements

1. **Integrated Property Search**
   - Custom IDX integration with real-time listings
   - Save favorites and search alerts
   - Map-based search with neighborhood overlays

2. **Smart Lead Capture**
   - Neighborhood guide downloads (gated content)
   - Home value estimator tool
   - Instant showing scheduling
   - Mortgage pre-qualification form

3. **Neighborhood Content Hub**
   - School ratings and reviews
   - Local amenities guide
   - Market statistics
   - Community event calendar

4. **Social Proof System**
   - Video testimonials from past clients
   - Market expertise data (homes sold, years experience)
   - Press and media features
   - Awards and certifications

5. **Luxury Marketing Features**
   - High-end video tours
   - Virtual reality walkthroughs
   - Drone photography integration
