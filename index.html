Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions. Can't find what you're looking for? Get in touch.

How long does it take to build a website?
Most websites are completed within 7-14 days, depending on complexity and content readiness. We provide a clear timeline during our initial consultation.
Will my website be mobile-friendly?
Absolutely. Every website we build is designed mobile-first and fully responsive. It will look and function perfectly on phones, tablets, and desktops.
Do you offer support after launch?
Yes! All packages include post-launch support. We also offer ongoing care plans for hosting, updates, backups, and monthly changes.
What do you need from me to get started?
We'll need your brand assets (logo, colors, images), content (text, pages), and any specific features or goals you want to achieve. We'll guide you through everything.
