Project Overview

This concept was developed for a fictional personal injury law firm in Houston, Texas. The firm specializes in car accidents, trucking accidents, and medical malpractice cases.

The strategic challenge: How do we create a digital presence that builds trust with potential clients and converts visitors into consultation requests?

Project Details

Industry
Legal / Personal Injury
Project Type
Strategic Concept
Focus Areas
Trust Building, Lead Capture, Credibility
Target Audience
Accident victims 25-55

Business Goals

  • Build trust with potential clients seeking legal representation
  • Increase consultation requests through strategic design and CTAs
  • Establish authority through case results and credentials
  • Capture leads 24/7 with online case evaluation forms

Website Strategy

Target Audience

Primary: Accident victims aged 25-55 seeking legal representation. Secondary: Family members of injury victims, referring attorneys.

Brand Positioning

Position the firm as Houston's trusted personal injury advocates—aggressive in the courtroom, compassionate with clients.
