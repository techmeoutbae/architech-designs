# Website Redesign Concept — Local Restaurant

## Concept Redesign — Not affiliated with business

---

## Business Type
**Local Restaurant** (Casual dining, family-style, or farm-to-table)

---

## Common Problems with Current Restaurant Websites

- **Outdated design** that doesn't reflect the current menu or brand atmosphere
- **Not mobile-friendly** — 60%+ of restaurant searches happen on mobile
- **Missing online ordering** integration for takeout and delivery
- **No way to make reservations** online
- **Slow loading times** cause potential customers to leave
- **No social proof** — missing reviews, photos, or testimonials
- **Outdated hours and information** that frustrates customers
- **Poor food photography** or no professional images at all
- **No SEO optimization** for local search "restaurants near me"
- **Missing menu accessibility** — no allergen info, vegan options clearly marked

---

## Redesign Strategy

### Brand Positioning
Position the restaurant as the neighborhood's go-to destination for great food and memorable experiences. Emphasize freshness, locally-sourced ingredients, and exceptional service.

### Key Strategic Elements

1. **Mobile-First Table Reservation System**
   - Integrate OpenTable, Resy, or custom reservation form
   - One-tap calling for reservations
   - Smart availability calendar

2. **Online Ordering Integration**
   - Seamless takeout and delivery ordering
   - Menu customization (mods, allergies)
   - Order tracking for customers

3. **Visual Food Storytelling**
   - Professional food photography throughout
   - Menu item high-resolution images
   - Behind-the-scenes chef content

4. **Local SEO Domination**
   - Google Business Profile optimization
   - Local keyword targeting
   - Review generation system

5. **Social Integration**
   - Instagram feed embedding
   - User-generated content gallery
   - Social proof through reviews

---

## New Website Structure

1. **Homepage**
   - Hero video or high-impact food imagery
   - Today's specials highlight
   - Quick actions: Order Online, Reserve Table
   - Featured dishes carousel
   - Hours, location, contact

2. **Menu Page**
   - Searchable, filterable menu
   - Item photos with descriptions
   - Allergen and dietary filters
   - Pricing and portions

3. **Our Story**
   - Chef's background
   - Restaurant history
   - Local sourcing philosophy

4. **Gallery**
   - Professional food photography
   - Interior atmosphere
   - Events and private dining

5. **Order Online**
   - Integrated ordering system
   - Pickup and delivery options
   - Payment processing

6. **Reserve a Table**
   - Reservation widget
   - Private dining inquiries
   - Event bookings

7. **Contact**
   - Location with map
   - Phone and email
   - Feedback form
