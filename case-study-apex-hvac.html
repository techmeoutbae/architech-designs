Project Overview

Apex Heating & Cooling is a well-established, reputable HVAC company serving Charlotte, North Carolina since 2012. With 12 technicians and a strong local reputation, they needed a website to match their service quality.

The challenge: They had no real website presence—only directory listings—and were losing potential customers to competitors with stronger digital presence. They needed to become the go-to HVAC company in Charlotte through digital dominance.

Project Details

Industry
HVAC / Home Services
Services
Strategy, Design, Development, Local SEO
Timeline
2 Weeks
Location
Charlotte, NC

Business Goals

  • Generate quality leads through a strategic, conversion-optimized website
  • Dominate local search for "HVAC repair near me" and similar high-intent keywords
  • Capture leads 24/7 with online quote request forms that work outside business hours
  • Build trust online with a professional website that showcases their established reputation

Website Strategy

Target Audience

Primary: Homeowners aged 30-55 needing HVAC repair or installation. Secondary: Property managers and landlords managing rental properties.

Brand Positioning

We positioned Apex as Charlotte's most trusted HVAC partner—the company that treats customers like family. Messaging emphasized their local, family-owned roots and commitment to transparent pricing.

Strategic Approach

  • Local SEO Domination: We optimized for exactly what local HVAC customers search for—"AC repair Charlotte," "furnace installation near me"—and built dedicated pages for each service.
  • 24/7 Lead Capture: Online quote request forms that capture detailed information, working around the clock to capture leads even when the office is closed.
  • Trust Building: Reviews, certifications, and guarantees prominently displayed to overcome the trust barrier common in home services.
  • Mobile-First Optimization: With 68% of searches on mobile, we ensured one-tap calling and seamless mobile experience.

Design Direction

Visual Style

We created a bold, trustworthy aesthetic that appeals directly to homeowners. The design uses strong colors and clear imagery to communicate reliability and professionalism without being corporate.

Real photos of technicians and trucks humanize the brand, while clean service icons make navigation intuitive.
