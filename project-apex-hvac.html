Project Overview

This concept was developed for a fictional established HVAC company in Charlotte, North Carolina. With 12 technicians and a strong local reputation since 2012, they needed a website to match their service quality.

The strategic challenge: How do we create a digital presence that generates quality leads 24/7 and dominates local search for high-value service keywords?

Project Details

Industry
HVAC / Home Services
Project Type
Strategic Concept
Focus Areas
Local SEO, Lead Generation, 24/7 Capture
Target Audience
Homeowners 30-55

Business Goals

  • Generate quality leads through a strategic, conversion-optimized website
  • Dominate local search for high-intent keywords like "HVAC repair near me"
  • Capture leads 24/7 with online quote request forms that work outside business hours
  • Build trust online with a professional website showcasing their established reputation

Website Strategy

Target Audience

Primary: Homeowners aged 30-55 needing HVAC repair or installation. Secondary: Property managers and landlords managing rental properties.

Brand Positioning

Position the company as Charlotte's most trusted HVAC partner—the company that treats customers like family with transparent pricing and reliable service.

Strategic Approach

  • Local SEO Domination: Optimize for exactly what local HVAC customers search for—"AC repair Charlotte," "furnace installation near me"—with dedicated pages for each service.
  • 24/7 Lead Capture: Online quote request forms that capture detailed information, working around the clock to capture leads even when the office is closed.
  • Trust Building: Reviews, certifications, and guarantees prominently displayed to overcome the trust barrier common in home services.
  • Mobile-First Optimization: With majority of searches on mobile, ensure one-tap calling and seamless mobile experience.

Design Direction

Visual Style

Create a bold, trustworthy aesthetic that appeals directly to homeowners. Use strong colors and clear imagery to communicate reliability and professionalism without being corporate.

Real photos of technicians and trucks humanize the brand, while clean service icons make navigation intuitive.
