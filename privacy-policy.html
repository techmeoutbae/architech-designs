Privacy Policy

Last Updated: April 2026

At Architech Designs, we value your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you visit our website or use our services.

1. Information We Collect

Personal Information We Collect:

  • Name
  • Email address
  • Phone number
  • Business name
  • Any information you voluntarily provide through our contact forms

Automatically Collected Information:

  • IP address
  • Browser type
  • Operating system
  • Access times
  • Pages viewed

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Respond to your inquiries
  • Provide the services you request
  • Communicate with you about our services
  • Improve our website and services
  • Comply with legal obligations

3. Information Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your personal information to outside parties except:

  • Service providers who assist us in operating our website
  • Legal authorities when required by law
  • Business transfer in the event of a merger or sale

4. Third-Party Services

Our website may contain links to third-party websites. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of these websites.

5. Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your personal information. However, no method of transmission over the Internet is 100% secure.

6. Cookies

Our website uses cookies to enhance your experience. You can set your browser to reject cookies.

7. Your Rights

You may request to:

  • Access your personal information
  • Correct inaccurate information
  • Request deletion of your information
  • Opt out of marketing communications

8. Children's Privacy

Our website is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

9. Contact Us

If you have questions about this policy, contact us at lillian@architechdesigns.net.
