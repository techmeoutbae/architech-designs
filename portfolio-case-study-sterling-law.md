# Portfolio Case Study - Sterling & Associates Law

## Project Overview

**Client:** Sterling & Associates Law  
**Industry:** Legal Services / Personal Injury Law  
**Project Type:** Website Redesign & Brand Transformation  
**Timeline:** 4 Weeks  
**Result:** 156% increase in consultation requests, 210% increase in organic traffic

---

## Business Profile

**Business Name:** Sterling & Associates  
**Location:** Houston, Texas  
**Practice Areas:** Personal injury, wrongful death, medical malpractice, trucking accidents  
**Established:** 2008  
**Team:** 4 attorneys, 8 paralegals, 6 administrative staff

---

## The Challenge

Sterling & Associates was a successful personal injury law firm with a strong reputation in Houston courts, but their online presence didn't reflect their legal expertise. Their website was:

- Outdated design from 2015
- Not mobile-friendly (critical since 60%+ of injury searches happen on phone)
- Missing case results and settlements (key trust signals)
- No system for capturing leads outside business hours
- Poor search visibility for high-value practice area keywords

Their goal: Establish digital presence as Houston's top personal injury firm and generate a consistent flow of consultation requests.

---

## Our Strategy

### Target Audience

Primary: Accident victims seeking legal representation (ages 25-55)  
Secondary: Family members of injury victims, referring attorneys

### Brand Positioning

We positioned Sterling & Associates as Houston's aggressive, results-driven personal injury team. The messaging emphasized:

- Millions in settlements recovered ($150M+ lifetime)
- No fees unless we win (contingency basis)
- 24/7 availability for accident victims
- Board-certified personal injury specialists
- Track record of maximum settlements

### Website Strategy

1. **Credibility-First Design:** Personal injury clients need to trust their attorney immediately. We built a site that communicates experience, results, and professionalism
2. **Case Results Showcase:** Prominent display of settlements and verdicts
3. **Trust-Building Content:** Informational pages about what to do after an accident
4. **Conversion-Focused:** Multiple paths to consultation requests
5. **Local SEO Authority:** Content and technical optimization for practice area terms

## What Made This Project Strong

1. **Results-Focused Content:** Personal injury clients want to know what the attorney can deliver. We built an extensive case results database that showcases the firm's track record.

2. **Trust Signal Integration:** We prominently displayed all credentials, certifications, and affiliations near conversion points. For injury attorneys, these trust signals are crucial.

3. **Lead Qualification System:** The case evaluation form captures enough information to pre-qualify leads and provides the attorney team with what they need to assess potential cases.

4. **SEO Authority Building:** We created comprehensive content for each practice area, targeting the exact terms potential clients search for when seeking legal help.

5. **24/7 Lead Capture:** By integrating live chat and optimizing forms for off-hours submissions, the firm now captures leads even when the office is closed.
