Cookie Policy

Last Updated: April 2026

This Cookie Policy explains what Cookies are and how Architech Designs uses them on our website.

1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your computer or mobile device when you visit websites. They help the website remember your actions and preferences over time.

2. How We Use Cookies

Our website uses cookies to:

  • Understand how visitors use our website
  • Improve your browsing experience
  • Analyze website traffic
  • Remember your preferences

3. Types of Cookies We Use

Essential Cookies:

These are necessary for the website to function. They cannot be switched off.

Analytics Cookies:

These help us understand how visitors interact with our website by collecting anonymous information.

Functional Cookies:

These allow the website to remember choices you make and provide enhanced features.

4. Managing Cookies

You can control or delete cookies through your browser settings. However, some features of our website may not work properly without cookies.

To manage cookies:

  • Chrome: Settings > Privacy > Cookies
  • Firefox: Options > Privacy > Cookies
  • Safari: Preferences > Privacy > Cookies
  • Edge: Settings > Privacy > Services

5. Third-Party Cookies

We may use third-party services that also set cookies, such as Google Analytics for analytics and social media platforms for sharing. We do not control these third-party cookies.

6. Contact

If you have questions about our use of cookies, contact us at lillian@architechdesigns.net.
