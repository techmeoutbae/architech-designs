## Medium Descriptions (For Expanded Cards)

### Luxe Medical Spa - Full Description

**Client:** Luxe Medical Spa | **Industry:** Medical Aesthetics  
**Services:** Custom Website Design, Brand Strategy, SEO, Booking Integration

We transformed a premium medical spa's online presence with a luxury-focused redesign. The challenge was to match their high-end in-clinic experience with their digital presence. We built trust through professional photography, strategically placed before/after galleries, and seamless online booking integration. The result: 47% more booking inquiries and 300% increase in mobile conversions.

**Key Results:**
- +47% Booking Inquiries
- +300% Mobile Conversion Rate
- +217% Online Booking Rate
- Page 1 Google Ranking

---

### Apex Heating & Cooling - Full Description

**Client:** Apex Heating & Cooling | **Industry:** Home Services / HVAC  
**Services:** Website Development, Local SEO, Lead Capture Systems, Brand Strategy

We built a lead generation powerhouse for this established HVAC company. Without a real website, they were invisible to local customers searching for services. We created a local SEO-dominant strategy with dedicated service pages, a robust quote request system, and trust-building elements. The result: 64% more service calls and 89% more online quote requests.

**Key Results:**
- +64% Service Calls
- +89% Quote Requests
- +1100% Website Traffic
- #3 Google "Near Me" Ranking

---

### Sterling & Associates Law - Full Description

**Client:** Sterling & Associates Law | **Industry:** Legal Services  
**Services:** Website Redesign, Brand Strategy, SEO, Lead Capture, Content Marketing

We transformed this established personal injury law firm's digital presence. With a dated website and poor mobile experience, they were losing leads to competitors. We built a trust-focused design showcasing their $150M+ in settlements, integrated 24/7 lead capture, and created comprehensive SEO content. The result: 156% more consultation requests and top 5 rankings for high-value keywords.

**Key Results:**
- +156% Consultation Requests
- +210% Organic Traffic
- #4 Google Ranking
- +324% Form Completion Rate
