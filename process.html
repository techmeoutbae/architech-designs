Our Process

How We Build Your Website

A simple, transparent process designed to get you results—without the confusion or surprises.

01

Free Strategy Call

We hop on a quick 30-minute call to understand your business, goals, and what you're looking for. No pressure, no obligation—just a conversation about how we can help.

02

Custom Proposal

You'll receive a detailed proposal with exactly what's included, the timeline, and the investment. Everything is clear and straightforward—no hidden fees.

03

Design You Approve

We create mockups of your homepage before we build anything. You see exactly what you're getting and can request changes—before we write a single line of code.

04

We Build It Right

Once you approve the design, we build your site with care. Mobile-friendly, fast-loading, and optimized for conversions—everything is handled professionally.

05

Your Approval

We share the completed site for your review. You get one round of revisions included. If something needs adjusting, we fix it—no hassle.

06

Launch & Celebrate

We handle the launch, set up your domain, SSL, and everything technical. Your site goes live—professional, fast, and ready to attract customers.
