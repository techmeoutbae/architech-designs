Founder & Medical Director

Dr. Sophia Chen

MD, FAAD | Medical Aesthetic Expert

Dr. Chen founded Lumina Beauty with a vision to combine medical excellence with luxury spa aesthetics. With over 15 years of experience in dermatology and medical aesthetics, she has helped thousands of clients achieve their beauty goals.

After completing her medical degree at UCLA and dermatology residency at Stanford, Dr. Chen pursued specialized training in injectables and laser treatments in Paris and Seoul. She brings together the best techniques from around the world to deliver exceptional, natural-looking results.
