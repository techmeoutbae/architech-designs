Questions

Frequently Asked

What should I expect during my first visit?

Your first visit includes a comprehensive consultation where we discuss your goals, assess your skin, and create a personalized treatment plan.

How long do results typically last?

Results vary by treatment. HydraFacials last 4-6 weeks, lash extensions 2-4 weeks with fills, Botox 3-4 months, and dermal fillers 6-18 months.

Is there downtime after treatments?

Most treatments have minimal to no downtime. You may experience slight redness or sensitivity which typically resolves within a day.

Do you offer payment plans?

Yes! We offer flexible payment plans through Cherry and CareCredit. We also have a membership program with exclusive discounts.
