# Portfolio Case Study - Apex Heating & Cooling

## Project Overview

**Client:** Apex Heating & Cooling  
**Industry:** HVAC / Home Services  
**Project Type:** New Website + Lead Generation System  
**Timeline:** 2 Weeks  
**Result:** 64% increase in service calls, 89% increase in quote requests

---

## Business Profile

**Business Name:** Apex Heating & Cooling  
**Location:** Charlotte, North Carolina  
**Services:** Residential HVAC installation, repair, maintenance, emergency services  
**Established:** 2012  
**Team:** 12 technicians, 4 office staff, 2 owners

---

## The Challenge

Apex Heating & Cooling was a well-established, reputable HVAC company but struggled to generate quality leads online. Their existing presence was minimal—a basic listing on a directory site—and they were losing potential customers to competitors with stronger digital presence.

**Key Issues:**
- No real website presence (only directory listings)
- Not showing up for local "HVAC repair near me" searches
- No way to capture leads outside business hours
- Competitors with polished websites were winning the trust battle
- No system for collecting and displaying customer reviews

Their goal: Become the go-to HVAC company in Charlotte through digital dominance.

---

## Our Strategy

### Target Audience

Primary: Homeowners aged 30-55 needing HVAC repair or installation  
Secondary: Property managers and landlords managing rental properties

### Brand Positioning

We positioned Apex as Charlotte's most trusted HVAC partner—the company that treats customers like family. The messaging emphasized:

- Local, family-owned business (since 2012)
- Same-day emergency service
- Upfront, transparent pricing
- Certified, background-checked technicians
- 100% satisfaction guarantee

### Website Strategy

1. **Local SEO Domination:** Optimized for "HVAC Charlotte," "AC repair near me," and other high-intent local keywords
2. **24/7 Lead Capture:** Online quote request forms that work around the clock
3. **Trust Building:** Reviews, certifications, and guarantees prominently displayed
4. **Service-Specific Landing Pages:** Dedicated pages for each service category
5. **Speed & Performance:** Fast-loading site for better UX and SEO

---

## Key Features

### Design Elements
- Bold, trustworthy aesthetic with blue and orange accents

- Service-specific pages (Installation, Repair, Maintenance, Emergency)
- Neighborhood landing pages (Myers Park, South End, etc.)
- HVAC guides and tips blog content
- Customer success stories with photos
- Video testimonials from happy clients

## Conversion Strategy

### Primary Goals
1. Online quote requests
2. Phone calls (click-to-call on mobile)
3. Maintenance plan sign-ups

### Implemented Tactics
- Sticky header with phone number always visible
- Prominent "Call Now" button on mobile
- Multiple quote form placements throughout site
- Exit-intent popup with special offer
- Live chat widget for instant questions
- Urgency messaging ("Only 2 appointments left this week")
- Trust signals near all conversion points

---

## What Made This Project Strong

1. **Local SEO Focus:** We targeted exactly what local HVAC customers search for—"AC repair Charlotte," "furnace installation near me"—and built pages specifically for those terms.

2. **Lead Capture Depth:** Rather than just a name/email form, we built a detailed quote system that captures enough information to pre-qualify leads and provide accurate estimates.

3. **Performance Marketing Integration:** The website was built to support paid advertising campaigns with dedicated landing pages for each service and offer.

4. **Social Proof Integration:** We created a systematic approach to collect and display reviews, which is crucial for home service businesses.

5. **Mobile Optimization:** With 68% of searches happening on mobile, we ensured every element works perfectly on smartphones—including one-tap calling.
